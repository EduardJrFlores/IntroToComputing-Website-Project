My Expertise

Map Control

Map control refers to a team's ability to dominate and influence different areas of the game map. It involves strategic positioning, vision control, and awareness to gain an advantage over the opponent.

Wide Champion Pool

A "wide champion pool" refers to a player's ability to proficiently play a diverse range of champions across different roles or positions in the game.

Wave Control

Wave control refers to the manipulation and management of minion waves to gain advantages in lane, exert pressure on opponents, and create opportunities for strategic plays.

Decision Making

Proper decision-making in League of Legends involves making strategic choices throughout the game that contribute to the overall success of your team.
